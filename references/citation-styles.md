## Bluebook (22nd Edition) — United States

### Cases
`Party v. Party, Volume Reporter Page (Court Year).`
- Example: *Marbury v. Madison*, 5 U.S. (1 Cranch) 137 (1803).
- Short form after first cite: *Marbury*, 5 U.S. at 140.

### Federal Statutes
`Title of Act, Pub. L. No. XX-XXX, § X, Stat. XXX (Year) (codified at X U.S.C. § X).`
- Short form: `X U.S.C. § X (Year).`

### Federal Regulations
`X C.F.R. § X.X (Year).`

### Law Review Articles
`Author First Last, *Title of Article*, Volume **Journal Abbreviation** Page (Year).`

### Key 22nd Edition Changes
- Italicize case names in law review footnotes; use regular type in court documents
- URLs required for online-only sources
- Signal "see" now requires explanatory parenthetical in most contexts

## OSCOLA — United Kingdom & Ireland

### Cases
`Party v Party [Year] Court Volume Report Page.`
- Example: *R v Cunningham* [1957] 2 QB 396.
- Neutral citation (post-2001): *Smith v Jones* [2010] EWCA Civ 123.

### Legislation
`Title Year, s Section(Sub).`
- Example: Companies Act 2006, s 172(1).

### Secondary Sources
`Author, *Title* (Edition Publisher Year) Page.`

---

## McGill Guide (9th Edition) — Canada

### Cases
`Style of Cause, Year Volume Reporter Series Page (Court).`
- Example: *R v Oakes*, [1986] 1 SCR 103 (SCC).

### Legislation
`Title, SOR/Year-No, s Section.` (regulations)
`Title, RSC Year, c Chapter, s Section.` (federal statutes)

---

## AGLC (4th Edition) — Australia

### Cases
`Party v Party (Year) Volume Report Page (Court).`
- Example: *Mabo v Queensland (No 2)* (1992) 175 CLR 1 (HCA).

### Legislation
`Title Year (Jurisdiction) s Section.`
- Example: *Corporations Act 2001* (Cth) s 180.

## Raw Hyperlink Format (Fallback)

Use when no jurisdiction-specific style applies or when the style is ambiguous.

**Format:**
`[Title of Document](URL), [Institution/Author], [Article/Section if applicable], [Date/Year].`

**Examples:**
- [Código Civil y Comercial de la Nación](https://www.argentina.gob.ar/normativa/nacional/ley-26994-235975), Ley 26.994, art. 1°, Argentina, 2014.
- [Directive 2016/679 (GDPR)](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32016R0679), European Parliament & Council, art. 5, 2016.

---

## General Rules (All Styles)

- Always hyperlink the source title when available
- Include the date of the source and the date accessed for web sources
- Mark any source you could not fully verify as **[UNVERIFIED]**
- For sources without a URL (paywalled or print-only), provide the full citation without a link and note "(subscription required)" or "(print)"
